# HRIS Audit Report and Security Recommendations

**Organization:** A Healthy Hand  
**Prepared by:** Internal Audit Department (James Wigfall)  
**Date:** September 27, 2023  

---

## Overview

This report presents the findings of an internal audit conducted on the Human Resources Information System (HRIS) recently deployed at A Healthy Hand. The audit evaluates system functionality, data integrity, and security controls to ensure alignment with organizational and compliance requirements.

---

## Scope

The audit focused on the following areas:

- Volunteer, employee, and technical staff data management  
- Accuracy and consistency of data entries  
- Security controls, particularly access management  
- Backup and disaster recovery processes  

---

## Audit Objective

The objective of this audit was to assess:

- Effectiveness of HRIS data management  
- Strength of security controls  
- Compliance with internal policies and external standards  

---

## Findings and Recommendations

---

### 1. Access Controls

**Issue:**  
The HRIS does not currently implement multi-factor authentication (MFA), increasing the risk of unauthorized access to sensitive employee and volunteer data.

**Recommendation:**  
Implement MFA to strengthen identity verification and protect sensitive information.

**Management Response:**  
The organization will coordinate with the system vendor to implement MFA by **Q2 (April 1, 2024)**.

---

### 2. Data Integrity and Accuracy

**Issue:**  
Discrepancies were identified between legacy system data and migrated HRIS records.

**Recommendation:**  
Conduct a comprehensive data validation process to ensure accuracy and consistency.

**Management Response:**  
The HR department will begin data verification efforts in **Q1 (October 1, 2023)**.

---

### 3. Backup and Recovery

**Issue:**  
No formal backup and recovery strategy has been documented.

**Recommendation:**  
Develop and document a backup and disaster recovery plan. Conduct periodic recovery testing to validate effectiveness.

**Management Response:**  
IT and HR teams will collaborate to establish a backup and recovery plan by **Q2 (April 1, 2024)**.

---

### 4. Vendor Support and SLA

**Issue:**  
Vendor support structure and update policies are not clearly defined.

**Recommendation:**  
Establish a Service Level Agreement (SLA) outlining:

- Support expectations  
- Update frequency  
- Response times for critical issues  

**Management Response:**  
The HR team will work with the vendor to finalize an SLA by **Q3 (July 1, 2024)**.

---

## Key Takeaways

- Strong access control (MFA) is essential for protecting sensitive HR data  
- Data accuracy is critical for operational reliability  
- Backup and recovery planning is necessary for business continuity  
- Vendor accountability must be clearly defined  

---

## Conclusion

The HRIS plays a critical role in supporting A Healthy Hand’s operations. While the system provides significant value, improvements in access control, data integrity, backup processes, and vendor management are necessary to strengthen security and reliability.

By implementing the recommended actions, the organization can enhance its security posture and ensure long-term operational success.
