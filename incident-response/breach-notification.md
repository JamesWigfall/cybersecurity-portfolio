# Data Breach Notification – Berwicq VIP Program

**Prepared by:** James Wigfall  

---

## Overview

This document provides a formal breach notification to affected customers following a security incident involving unauthorized access to Berwicq’s database. The goal is to ensure transparency, inform impacted individuals, and outline steps taken to mitigate risk.

---

## Incident Summary

Berwicq recently identified a security incident involving unauthorized access to internal systems.

### Key Details

- Unauthorized access to proprietary database  
- Exposure of sensitive customer data  
- Leak of internal product-related information  

---

## Data Impacted

The breach affected members of the Berwicq VIP program.

### Information Exposed

- Blood pressure readings  
- Cholesterol levels  
- Body Mass Index (BMI)  

This information is considered sensitive and requires careful handling to prevent misuse.

---

## Response Actions

Upon discovery of the incident, the following actions were taken:

- Secured affected systems and restricted unauthorized access  
- Engaged internal and third-party cybersecurity experts  
- Initiated a full security review and remediation process  
- Implemented enhanced security controls and monitoring  
- Notified law enforcement and began cooperation with investigations  

---

## Customer Guidance

Affected individuals are encouraged to take the following precautions:

### Recommended Actions

1. Monitor personal and health-related information for suspicious activity  
2. Report any unusual activity to appropriate authorities  
3. Update account credentials immediately  
4. Enable multi-factor authentication (MFA) where available  
5. Consider identity protection or monitoring services
